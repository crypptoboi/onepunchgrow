Saitama Grow Trading Ecosystem

Saitama Grow smart contract applies a 8% fee on all transactions (buys/sells). Out of this, 2% is automatically distributed to holders, without farming! This means the number of tokens in your wallet will forever increase as people transact. In addition, the Saitama Grow smart contract also takes 1% of each transaction and automatically adds it to the liquidity pool.

The liquidity pool will continuously increase, which leads to a higher price floor for Saitama Grow.
